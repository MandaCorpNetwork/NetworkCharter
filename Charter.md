# 1. GENERAL PURPOSE / MISSION STATEMENT

**The Unnamed Alliance (the "Alliance")** exists to create a **secure, collaborative, and mutually beneficial network of player-run organisations** across *Star Citizen*. Our core objectives are to:

1. **Promote collective security** - defend member territories, share intelligence, and coordinate responses to external threats.__
2. **Foster economic prosperity** - enable free, fair, mutually benificial, and trasparent trade, resource sharing, and joint infrastructure projects.__
3. **Encourage community growth** - provide a welcoming environment for new players, mentor newcomers, and assist players in finding a group that inspires them to have higher playtime and participation rates.__
4. **Provide Collective Intercommunication** - a system of communication between organisations, individuals and crews of people for consistent and connected gameplay.__
5. **Centralize Loyalty** - Ensuring all organisations, groups and individuals have a strong loyalty to the larger collective.

*All actions taken by the Alliance and its members must further these aims.*__

## 2. GENERAL COMMITMENTS

### 2.1. Aid
* **Defensive Aid** - Members agree to provide timely assistance (troops, reinforcements, or strategic support [as available]) when any member is under attack, provided the request follows the **Aid Request Procedure** (Section 4.1).__
* **Humanitarian Aid** - In the event of a catastrophic loss of resources (e.g., a raide that destroys a major base), a member may request emergency supplies. The Alliance will allocate a **relief pool** on a rotating-priority basis, suplemented by the Central Hub Bank.__
* **Administrative Aid** - For groups or organisations that request, the Hub will provide Administrative tools, training & infrastructure to assist with Individual Community Management and Operations.__
* **Relations Aid** - Assist in conflict resolution, outreach, and public relations on the behalf of other members.__

### 2.2. Economic Partner
* **Open-Market Principle** - All members may trade any in-game commodity or resource unless explicityle prohibited by the Code of Conduct (Section 3).__
* **Resource-Sharing Pools** - Each member may contribute a voluntary percentage of their surplus to the **Common Supply Depot** that can be drawn upon by any member with a valid request.__
* **Joint Projects** - The Alliance may establish **Base-Sharing Agreements**, **Mining/Harvesting Coalitions**, or **Infrastructure Networks** (e.g., shared transport routes). Such projects must be approved via the **Project Approval Process** (Section 4.5).__
* **Central Hub Bank** - The Alliance Hub will maintain a Central Bank system that maintains an Alliance Treasury that maintains the Common Supply Depot, Central Currency Bank, & Alliance Stocks.

### 2.3. Community Growth Support
* **New-Commer Support** - New Members to any Organisations should be treated as individuals, with their highest level of enjoyment of participation taken into mind when recruiting or interacting with.__
* **Member Playstyle Preference** - Player Playstyle Prefernce should be taken into account when attempting to Onboard New Members to individuals Member Orgs. If there is a better Org, Crew, or Group that may suit their playstyle more, they should be properly directed to those communities.__
* **Player Relocation** - If a player has requested to switch their Primary Org Membership to another Member Org, the player should be allowed to do so if the Org they are requested to move to has given proper internal sign-offs.__
* **Event Participation** - Member Orgs are to participate in Alliance Level Events & Member Org Hosted Events when able.__
* **Community Access** - All Member Orgs should Maintain a Directory of Member Org Communites, Hub Website & Tools, Shared Training Documentation, and Intercommunication Channels that all certified members have access to.__
